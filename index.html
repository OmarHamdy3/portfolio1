Road Accident Analysis Dashboard

Project Description

This interactive dashboard analyzes 5 years of national road accident data to identify high-risk locations, times, and contributing factors for safety planning. The project was commissioned by the national transportation authority.

Project Video

Dashboard Views

Key Findings

  • Friday evenings between 6-9pm accounted for 28% of all accidents
  • 3 specific highway intersections were responsible for 15% of fatal accidents
  • Rainy conditions increased accident severity by 42%
  • Young drivers (18-25) were involved in 38% of accidents despite being 15% of drivers

Recommendations

  • Increase police patrols during high-risk times
  • Redesign the 3 dangerous intersections
  • Implement weather-based speed limit adjustments
  • Develop targeted driver education programs for young drivers
